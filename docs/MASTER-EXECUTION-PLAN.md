## Risk Analysis & Mitigation

### Risk #1: No Responses to Outreach (30% probability)

**If 0 of 4 respond:**
- Fallback A: Public Moltbook post (m/infrastructure)
- Fallback B: LaunchClaw pitch (bypass direct outreach)
- Fallback C: USDC hackathon retroactive showcase

**Mitigation:**
- Fix landing page BEFORE outreach (first impression matters)
- Personalize messages (reference their specific posts/work)
- Follow up at 48h, 7d (persistence pays)

---

### Risk #2: Demo Calls Don't Convert (40% probability)

**If 3 demos, 0 signups:**
- Problem: Pitch isn't resonating
- Solution: Ask "What would make this a yes?"
- Learn: Objections, refine pitch
- Iterate: Adjust value prop, pricing, timeline

**Mitigation:**
- Record demo calls (with permission)
- Review what worked/didn't
- A/B test different pitches
- Offer: More generous free trial (2 months vs 1)

---

### Risk #3: Integration Takes Longer Than 2 Weeks (50% probability)

**If first integration takes 4 weeks:**
- Problem: Over-promised timeline
- Solution: Honest communication ("learning curve, here's what we found")
- Update: Change "2 weeks" to "2-4 weeks" for future pitches

**Mitigation:**
- Integration testing BEFORE outreach (validate 2-week claim)
- Over-support (daily check-ins, immediate bug fixes)
- Document blockers (what slowed us down? how to avoid?)

---

### Risk #4: Charles/Archil Ships First (20% probability)

**If competitor launches before we close customers:**
- Problem: Lost first-mover advantage
- Solution: Emphasize OUR proof (Feb 9 loan vs their launch)
- Differentiate: Better support, faster integration, proven results

**Mitigation:**
- Move fast (fix gaps in 2 days, outreach by Day 3)
- Close first customer in 2 weeks (before competitor can copy)
- Network effects: First platform = data moat

---

### Risk #5: Technical Issues During Demo (30% probability)

**If API crashes during demo call:**
- Problem: Lost credibility ("it doesn't work")
- Solution: Pre-record demo video (backup)
- Offer: "Let me show you the recording, then we'll debug live after"

**Mitigation:**
- Test demo environment thoroughly (Day 1)
- Have backup plan (screen recording)
- Practice demo 3x before first call
