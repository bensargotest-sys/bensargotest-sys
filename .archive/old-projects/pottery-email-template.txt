2. Pricing Confidence
£50-100 is FAIR for London handmade ceramics. You're not competing with Ikea. Not On The High Street potters charge £28-55 wholesale (maker gets 50% = £14-27). Your direct pricing is appropriate.

Collect at markets, add to Instagram bio. Email converts 10x better than social media. You own the list (algorithm-proof).
